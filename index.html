My Story

The darkest rooms
teach the clearest lessons.

"The hardest chapter of my life became the most powerful lesson I could ever share with a leader."

I built businesses. I led teams. I made decisions worth millions.

Then I lost everything — not in a boardroom, but behind a locked door. That experience stripped away every pretence about what leadership actually is.

What I found on the other side wasn't just survival. It was a level of clarity, resilience and strategic thinking that no MBA, no mentor, no corporate career could have given me. Today I bring that truth into rooms where polished advice has stopped working.
