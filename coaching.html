My Approach

Why this coaching
is different.

Most coaches learned their frameworks from other coaches, or from business schools. The knowledge is real — but it has never been tested in the conditions that truly reveal a leader's character.

I did not learn resilience in a workshop. I learned it in a prison cell, after losing everything I had spent seven years building. I did not learn operational leadership in a classroom. I lead a manufacturing business from the board today.

That combination — extreme lived adversity plus active executive experience — is what makes the coaching I offer genuinely different from anything else available in the UK market.

No templates
Every engagement is built entirely around your situation. Nothing off the shelf.
Radical honesty
I will tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. That is what real progress requires.
Real accountability
Not a cheerleader. A thinking partner who challenges, tracks, and holds you to the standard you set yourself.
Proven under pressure
The frameworks I share have been tested in conditions far beyond the boardroom. They work when it matters most.
